Mission & Vision

Our Vision

Our group's vision is that every addict can have the opportunity to experience the NA message- “ An addict, any addict, can stop using drugs, lose the desire to use, and find a new way to live.”

Our Mission

Our group is carrying the NA message by practicing and sharing NA principles in everything we do. We are also carrying the message through creating a recovery atmosphere where newcomers feel welcome
and at home.

Meetings

Every Sunday 8:00pm-9:30pm (GMT+6:00) (Bangladesh Standard Time)

Meeting Format

1st Sunday of the month - Literature Reading/Topic Discussion


3rd Sunday of the month - Speaker Meeting


Rest of the Sundays of the month - General Sharing Meeting based on the topic of Just For Today Daily Meditations

Location

Narcotics Anonymous

NA is a nonprofit fellowship or society of men and women for whom drugs had become a major problem. We are recovering addicts who meet regularly to help each other stay clean. This is a program of complete abstinence from all drugs. There is only one requirement for membership, the desire to stop using.
