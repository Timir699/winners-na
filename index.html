Our Mission & Vision

Vision statement

our groups vision is that every addict have the opportunity to experience NA message- “ An addict, any addict, can stop using drugs, lose the desire to use, and find a new way to live”

Mission statement

Mission statement: our group is carrying the NA message by practicing and sharing NA principles in everything we do. We are also carrying the message through creating a recovering atmosphere where newcomers feel welcome and at home

Meetings

Every Sunday 8:00-9:30pm(GMT+6 Bangladesh Standard Time)

Meeting Format

1st Sunday of the month - Literature Reading/Topic Discussion

3rd Sunday of the month - Speaker Meeting

Rest of the Sundays of the month - General Sharing Meeting based on the topic of Just For Today Daily Meditations

Narcotics Anonymous

NA is a nonprofit fellowship or society of men and women for whom drugs had become a major problem. We are recovering addicts who meet regularly to help each other stay clean. This is a program of complete abstinence from all drugs. There is only one requirement for membership, the desire to stop using.
